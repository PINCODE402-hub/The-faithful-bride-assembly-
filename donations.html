Why We Give

At Faith Church, we believe giving is an act of worship. It's a tangible expression of trust in God's provision and a partnership in the work of the Gospel.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." — 2 Corinthians 9:7

Is it secure?

Yes. All transactions are securely encrypted and processed by leading enterprise payment gateways. Your financial details are never stored on our church servers.
